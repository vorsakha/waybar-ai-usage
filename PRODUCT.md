# Product

## Register

product

## Users

A single Omarchy desktop user who regularly works with Claude and Codex and needs to understand remaining quota without interrupting their current task or opening either CLI.

## Product Purpose

Surface current Claude and Codex quota in Waybar, then reveal the full 5-hour and weekly picture in one click. Success means the user can understand provider health, usage, reset timing, and data freshness in under two seconds.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, precise, native. The tool should feel like a considered part of the desktop rather than a separate dashboard.

## Anti-references

Avoid SaaS dashboard styling, decorative gradients, glass effects, oversized metrics, excessive cards, gratuitous motion, and color-only status communication.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the bar calm; put detail behind intent.
2. Make freshness and failure visible, never silently stale.
3. Use the desktop theme as the source of truth.
4. Return cached information immediately, then refresh in the background.
5. Prefer familiar controls and readable labels over invented affordances.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain readable contrast, pair status color with text, support keyboard Escape, keep motion functional and brief, and remain usable when either provider is unavailable.
